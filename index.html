I am skilled in AI/ML tuning and delivering actionable insights, optimizing models for real-world challenges. Currently, I am a PhD candidate at Utrecht University, where I focus on Explainable AI, Reinforcement Learning, and Cognitive Science, bridging the gap between AI and social science. I am passionate about ethical AI and advocate for Human-AI collaboration. My expertise encompasses finance, fraud detection, and NLP, where I leverage deep learning to optimize AI performance. With an MSc in Industrial Engineering, I apply advanced machine learning and statistical modeling to tackle complex problems. I excel in data visualization using tools like Tableau, Power BI, and D3.js, translating data insights into clear, actionable strategies. I am technically proficient in Python, R, SQL, TensorFlow, PyTorch, and cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, and GCP. As an active researcher and speaker, I contribute to open-source projects and participate in AI competitions. In my previous role, I led a team in developing a recommendation engine that increased user engagement by 25%. As a strong communicator, I present complex findings to diverse audiences and collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams. I am committed to continuous learning and ensure that I remain at the forefront of AI innovations.
