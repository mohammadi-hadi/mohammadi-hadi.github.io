Abstract

The rapid advancement of Natural Language Processing (NLP) models, particularly advanced models like GPT and BERT, has led to their widespread adoption in critical decision-making across healthcare, finance, and customer relationship management. However, the black-box nature of these advanced NLP models has created an urgent need for transparency and trustworthiness.

This survey examines how explainable NLP (XNLP) can be deployed in real-world contexts while addressing domain-specific challenges. We focus on underexplored areas including real-world applicability, evaluation metrics, and human interaction in model assessment.

Our work aims to bridge knowledge gaps in XNLP literature through domain-specific exploration, addressing practical deployment challenges, and suggesting future research directions for broader XNLP application.

Survey Methodology

Literature Collection

Systematic search across major databases (ACL Anthology, IEEE Xplore, arXiv) for papers published between 2020-2024 on explainable NLP.

Paper Selection

Applied strict inclusion criteria focusing on papers with practical implementations and empirical evaluations of explainability methods.

Categorization

Developed a multi-dimensional taxonomy to categorize papers based on explainability approach, application domain, and evaluation methods.

Analysis & Synthesis

Conducted in-depth analysis to identify patterns, gaps, and emerging trends in explainable NLP across different domains.

Key Findings

Major Insights

1. Critical Domain Applications: Advanced NLP models are increasingly used in high-stakes domains including healthcare, finance, and customer relationship management, where transparency is essential.

2. Transparency Imperative: The black-box nature of models like GPT and BERT creates an urgent need for explainability in real-world deployments.

3. Underexplored Areas: Key gaps exist in real-world applicability of XNLP methods, standardized evaluation metrics, and human interaction in model assessment.

4. Domain-Specific Challenges: Different application domains require tailored explainability approaches to address their unique requirements and constraints.

Research Impact

This survey has significant implications for the future development of explainable NLP systems. By providing a comprehensive overview of the current landscape, we enable researchers to:

  • Identify underexplored areas and research opportunities
  • Understand domain-specific requirements for explainability
  • Apply best practices from successful implementations
  • Avoid common pitfalls in developing explainable systems

Our work serves as a foundation for future research in explainable NLP, promoting the development of more transparent, trustworthy, and user-friendly AI systems across various application domains.
